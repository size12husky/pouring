Pouring!

Instructions

Controls

WASD to move

P to open shop

M to fire turret

Gameplay

- Avoid falling obstacles and collect buckets

- 20 buckets gets you to the next level!

- Buckets turn to coins before they disappear. Collect coins to buy power-ups in the shop!

- Obstacles fall faster each level

- Look out for new obstacles as you progress!

Power-Ups

  • Bowl, Bone, and Beethoven

    These power-ups are a one-time defense against a certain obstacle. Purchase up to 3 in the shop and activate by clicking their icon in the top-right of the screen.

  • Laser Defense System

    Purchase up to 3 laser defense codes in the shop. Activate by clicking the icon in the top-right of the screen. Activating will disable lasers for 15 seconds.

  • Turret

    Activate the turret by purchasing it in the shop! Fire the Turret with M. Enemies hit with the turret will drop coins!
